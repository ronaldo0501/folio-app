What Folio does

Everything a great journal needs.

Built around how people actually write — simple to start, powerful when you need it.

✦
AI Writing Assistant

Tap once to polish your writing, fix grammar, or expand your thoughts. Your original is always preserved — you decide what to keep.

🔍
Search Everything

Find any entry by words, tags, or what's in your photos. Our AI describes every photo you attach so you can search for "waterfall" and find it instantly.

📷
Photo Journals

Attach photos to your entries. Folio automatically understands what's in each photo so you can find memories by what you saw, not just what you wrote.

🔒
Private by Design

Locked with Face ID or Touch ID. Backed up to your private iCloud. No ads. No analytics. No one reads your journal but you.

😊
Mood Tracking

Log how you're feeling with each entry. Filter and search by mood to spot patterns in your emotional life over time.

📤
Export Anytime

Your data is always yours. Export your entire journal as PDF, JSON, or Markdown whenever you want. No lock-in, ever.

Your privacy matters

Built for trust.

A journal is one of the most personal things you can keep. We treat it that way.

🔒

Local First

Your entries live on your device first. CloudKit backup goes to your personal iCloud — not our servers.

🚫

No Ads. Ever.

Folio will never show ads or sell your data. We make money from Premium subscriptions only.

👁️

We Can't Read It

Your journal is stored in your private iCloud container. Technically impossible for us to access.

📦

Always Exportable

Export everything anytime. Your memories don't belong to an app — they belong to you.
